This course is designed for students who meet the criteria to compete in intercollegiate basketball. All
varsity sports entail practice at designated times. Since skills/proficiencies are enhanced by supervised
repetition and practice, this course may be repeated three times.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. define basketball safety and rules.
B. practice the proper etiquette of basketball competition.
C. describe offensive and defensive strategies for intercollegiate competition.
D. perform offensive and defensive skills at a level appropriate for intecollegiate competition.
E. demonstrate pre-game, in-game and post-game physical routines.
F. demonstrate proper maintenance of individual equipment and the court.

VI. EXAMPLES OF ASSIGNMENTS
A. Reading Assignments
1. Research an internet article that outlines the history of basketball and the impact the sport
has had on society. Plan to make a brief presentation in class.
2. Review opponent scouting reports and specific game strategy. Be prepared to discuss your
findings in class.
B. Writing Assignments
1. Write an essay that reviews and analyzes your last three game performances.
2. Write a brief paper summarizing the responsibilities of each position in basketball.
C. Out-of-Class Assignments
1. Develop a set of personal goals for the season and list three goals you feel the team should
adopt. Team goal suggestions will be discussed in class.
2. View a professional basketball event and report your observations as they address
conduct, rules, and techniques.
