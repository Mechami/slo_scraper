COURSE OUTLINE
I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
AJLE 150 - Level I Module Academy Preparation Review
Prerequisite(s): (See Catalog for Program Admission Criteria)
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
16 hours Lecture
16 hours Lab

1.25 Unit(s)

This 32-hour course prepares students who have completed Module III and Module II of the
California Commission on Peace Officer Standards and Training (P.O.S.T.) Regular Basic Academy
Course to participate in the Butte College Module I Academy Course. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Demonstrate an understanding of P.O.S.T. Learning Domains 1 (Professionalism and Ethics); 2
(Criminal Justice System); 3 (Community Relations); and 5 (Introduction to Criminal Law).
B. Demonstrate an understanding of P.O.S.T. Learning Domains 6 (Crimes Against Property); 7
(Crimes Against Persons); and 8 (General Criminal Statutes).
C. Demonstrate an understanding of P.O.S.T. Learning Domains 15 (Laws of Arrest); 16 (Search
and Seizure); and 20 (Use of Force).
D. Demonstrate an understanding of P.O.S.T. Learning Domains 31 (Custody); 36 (Information
Systems); and 39 (Crimes Against the Justice System).
