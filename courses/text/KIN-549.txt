COURSE OUTLINE
I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
KIN 40 - Jazz Dance
Prerequisite(s): NONE
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: CSU/UC
17 hours Lecture
34 hours Lab

1.5 Unit(s)

This course provides instruction centered upon improving the knowledge and physical techniques of
jazz dance. The course will identify jazz dance styles: lyrical, contemporary, and character/musical
theater. Basic jazz dance techniques, choreography, and vocabulary will be applied and practiced.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. identify and perform the basic techniques of jazz dance.
B. identify and demonstrate the lyrical, contemporary, character/musical theater styles of jazz
dance.
C. explain jazz dance rules with proper jazz dance vocabulary.
D. demonstrate improvement of the skills of jazz dance.

VI. EXAMPLES OF ASSIGNMENTS
A. Reading Assignments
1. Read and be prepared to discuss the handout on the history of jazz dance.
2. Read and be prepared to discuss the handout on techniques of jazz dance.
B. Writing Assignments
1. Design and submit a one minute jazz dance sequence.
2. Write a one page essay describing the safety factors in jazz dance training and
performance.
C. Out-of-Class Assignments
1. View through media or attend a jazz dance performance and submit a written overview of
the performance.
2. Attend a theater event in the community that includes dance in the performance and be
prepared to discuss the event in class.
