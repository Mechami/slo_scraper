II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. recognize and repeat a variety of phonetic and spelling patterns
B. identify imperative mood, irregular verbs and infinitives
C. combine sentences using and
D. construct simple sentences in writing
E. express effective verbal responses to questions
F. utilize appropriate language responses in common social contexts
G. apply life-skill topics in real-life situations
H. compare various cultural perspectives
I. repeat, discuss, and answer questions to a short reading selection
