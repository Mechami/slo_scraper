I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
COS 100 - Introduction to Cosmetology
Prerequisite(s): High School Diploma or G.E.D.
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
30 hours Lecture
210 hours Lab

5 Unit(s)

This course is the first in a seven-course sequence. It introduces students to the history of
Cosmetology, career opportunities in Cosmetology, and the skills required to have a successful career
in the beauty industry. Students will learn the basic skills and techniques required for all aspects of
hair care, hair cutting, hair treatments, makeup application, skin care and nail care and the safe,
hygienic practice of Cosmetology procedures. Additionally, students will learn and develop basic
skills in human relations, customer satisfaction and the professional attitudes that are vital to success
in the beauty industry.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Describe the general objectives of the Cosmetology program.
B. Explain the completion and licensure requirements to practice Cosmetology in California.
C. Explain the importance of consumer safety and general safety procedures.
D. List the principles that contribute to personal and professional success.
E. Demonstrate an understanding of ergonomic principles and ergonomically correct postures and
movements.
F. List the methods used for Universal Precautions and infection control.
G. Explain the differences between cleaning, disinfecting, and sterilizing.
H. Complete basic set up for different hair care procedures.
I. Identify different implements and their proper manipulation.
J. Identify the correct draping techniques for different salon services.

VI. EXAMPLES OF ASSIGNMENTS
A. Reading Assignments
1. Read an article about industry trends in a professional magazine such as American
Hairdresser, Modern Salon or Behind the Chair. Be prepared to give a 3 minute
presentation to the class focused on industry trends.
2. Read the Cosmetology Act, Rules and Regulations, as published by the Department of
Consumer Affairs. Prepare a 7 slide PowerPoint that covers key aspects of this legislation.
B. Writing Assignments
1. Write a 1-2 page essay focused on the essential communication, time management, and
financial skills necessary to manage a successful salon.
2. Write a 1-2 page essay on the origins and history of the Cosmetology industry.
C. Out-of-Class Assignments
1. Attend a Cosmetology conference and prepare a short presentation for your classmates
focused on what you learned.
2. Interview 2-3 salon owners focusing on key salon management strategies. Be prepared to
present your findings in class.
