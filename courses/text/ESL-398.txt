I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
ESL 230 - ESL Level 4-Academic
Prerequisite(s): ESL 313 or qualifying eligibility from the ESL Assessment.
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
51 hours Lecture

3 Unit(s)

This course provides non-native English speakers with the skills needed to improve and integrate a
student's basic academic reading, writing, listening, and speaking. The course also introduces
intermediate level grammar structures in context. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. recognize and apply basic grammar and punctuation conventions
B. formulate simple written paragraphs
C. comprehend academic text at the appropriate level
D. use appropriate academic vocabulary in writing and speech
E. respond appropriately to oral command and answer oral questions correctly
F. compose and present to the class a short oral presentation
G. apply test-taking and study skills to other courses
