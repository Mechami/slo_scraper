COURSE OUTLINE
I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
ESL 254 - ESL Reading Workshop
3 Unit(s)
Prerequisite(s): Qualifying eligibility for ESL Level IV based on ESL assessment or
ESL 313
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
51 hours Lecture
This course emphasizes vocabulary building, including development of sight vocabulary, phonics
instruction and structural analysis, and improving comprehension. Instruction will rely heavily on the
student's existing knowledge of language and will stress the integration of skills into language as a
whole. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Interpret and follow simple oral and written directions.
B. Recognize the relationship of reading to language as a whole.
C. Identify the main idea, supporting details and conclusion of a level-appropriate text.
D. Demonstrate an understanding of basic social and academic skills necessary for college success.
E. Present orally from a level-appropriate reading.
