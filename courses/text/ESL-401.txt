I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
ESL 233 - ESL Listening/Speaking 1
Prerequisite(s): ESL 313 or qualifying eligibility from the ESL Assessment.
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
34 hours Lecture

2 Unit(s)

This course assists the non-native English speaking student in achieving effective personal
communication. Self-improvement involving speech fundamentals and academic language is
emphasized. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. recognize the sound patterns and qualities of English
B. respond correctly to oral questions and follow spoken directions
C. formulate and ask questions in English
D. assess and refine his/her English pronunciation
E. express herself/himself orally with appropriate level fluency
F. compose and deliver a short speech
