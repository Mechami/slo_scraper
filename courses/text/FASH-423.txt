I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
FASH 74 - Historic Costume
Prerequisite(s): NONE
Recommended Prep: Reading Level IV; English Level III
Transfer Status: CSU
51 hours Lecture

3 Unit(s)

This course introduces students to the history of clothing and costume including the influence of
historic attire on current fashion apparel. The course examines the impacts of social class, religion and
political conditions on expression through dress.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Classify and compare the specific characteristics of costume from various periods.
B. Describe and analyze the development, origin, and interrelationship of various clothing styles in
relation to their specific cultures and purposes during that time of history when they were most
popular.
C. Identify the key characteristics of fashion in a variety of historic periods, and analyze the
influence historic fashions have on modern style.
D. Examine the social, political, religious, and economic conditions of different historic periods as
they relate to clothing.

VI. EXAMPLES OF ASSIGNMENTS
A. Reading Assignments
1. Read the chapter on Egyptian Costume in the ”Survey of Historic Costume” text. Be
prepared to discuss the elements of Egyptian costume, and participate in a hands-on
activity to create and wear ancient Egyptian garments.
2. Read the chapter on Post World War II and New Look Fashion, included in the “Survey
of Historic Costume” text. Be prepared to discuss the characteristics of post WWII
fashions and the socio-political influences upon fashion at that time, and how these differ
from those of the 1920’s, 1930’s and WWII.
B. Writing Assignments
1. Write a 5-7 page paper about the clothing of an historical era of your choice, and include
discussion of the textile technologies, political, social, artistic, economic aspects of the
period.
2. Write a 4 page paper about the costumes developed for a period film of your choice.
Assess the overall quality of the costumes, and their historical accuracy based on your
research.
C. Out-of-Class Assignments
1. Use the Internet to research the clothing of an historical era as well as the work of current
designers to find examples that have been influenced by that period. Create a PowerPoint
presentation with images that compare and contrast the historic garments with those of the
present.
2. Summarize your experience during the field trip to a theatre costume shop and report on
some of the aspects of costuming and garments that are unique to theater, or costume
preservation.
