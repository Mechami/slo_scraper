I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
GEOG 12 - The American West
Prerequisite(s): NONE
Recommended Prep: Reading Level IV; English Level IV
Transfer Status: CSU/UC
51 hours Lecture

3 Unit(s)

A topical survey of the American West from the Great Plains to the Pacific, emphasizing both
historical and contemporary interpretations of settlement, resource management, economic
development, and urbanization. Particular emphasis is placed on how various cultural groups with
differing social, political, and religious values have each made a unique impact upon the western
landscape.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. analyze the physical character of the landscape.
B. recognize migration and settlement patterns of various cultural groups.
C. describe the development of regional interconnectedness and economic dependency.
D. compare and contrast urban and rural land use policies.
E. formulate reasoned opinions about the future of the American West.
