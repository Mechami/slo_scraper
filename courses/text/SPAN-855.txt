I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
SPAN 3 - Third Semester Spanish
Prerequisite(s): SPAN 2

Transfer Status: CSU/UC
68 hours Lecture

4 Unit(s)

This course is the first half of intermediate Spanish which further develops the skills of understanding,
speaking, reading, and writing within a culturally rich context. It is a review and further study of
structural elements. Reading selections from the Hispanic world will be included. Students who have
completed at least two, but fewer than three years of high school Spanish (or equivalent) with a letter
grade of "A" or "B" within one year of the present date, should register for Fourth Semester Spanish.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. comprehend the Spanish language more successfully in the following contexts as it is used by
native speakers: business Settings, quotidian community life, familiar settings (e.g., with family
and friends)
B. speak Spanish with increased proficiency and accuracy
C. speak Spanish with improved rhythm, intonation, and stress patterns
D. demonstrate correct and idiomatic speaking habits
E. demonstrate improved reading skills in Spanish with direct comprehension, i.e., without
conscious translation
F. demonstrate Spanish writing skills
G. compare and contrast elements of the cultures of Spanish-speaking regions
