I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
ESL 232 - ESL Level 6-Academic
Prerequisite(s): ESL 231 or qualifying eligibility from the ESL Assessment.
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
51 hours Lecture

3 Unit(s)

This course provides non-native speakers of English with instruction and practice in writing, speaking,
and reading skills at an advanced level. Strong emphasis is placed upon written communication,
especially refining sentence structure and polishing the paragraph. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. demonstrate increased fluency, confidency, and accuracy in oral and written English
B. comprehend, analyze and critique readings at appropriate level
C. apply critical listening skills to improve comprehension
D. use appropriate vocabulary and language conventions to effectively communicate orally
E. utilize library skills to research material for short paper
F. compose and present orally a short paper
G. apply note-taking, study strategies, and test-taking strategies to improve academic performance
