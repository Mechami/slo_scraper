I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
COS 104 - Cosmetology IV
Prerequisite(s): COS 103 or Permission of Instructor
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
30 hours Lecture
210 hours Lab

5 Unit(s)

This course is the fifth in a seven-course sequence. Students are introduced to hair removal, skin
analysis and care, facial massage and makeup application. Emphasis will be placed on temporary and
permanent hair removal, skin typing and treatments, facial massage, proper use of electrotherapy and
light therapy, color theory and principles of makeup application. Students will continue developing the
required skills and techniques for all aspects of hair care, hair cutting, hair treatments, makeup
application, skin care and nail care and the safe, hygienic practice of Cosmetology procedures.
Additionally, students will continue developing skills in human relations, customer satisfaction and
the professional attitudes that are vital to success in the beauty industry.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Perform client consultations for hair removal and skin analysis/treatments.
B. Apply proper procedure for hair removal when using hard and soft wax.
C. Demonstrate proper setup and draping procedures for skin care and hair removal services.
D. Name the conditions that contraindicate hair removal in the salon.
E. Recognize the different skin types and demonstrate corresponding procedures for skin care
correction treatments.
F. Based on an evaluation of skin condition, demonstrate proper facial massage techniques.
G. Safely use electric and light therapy treatments for cosmetic services.
H. Demonstrate use of color theory when applying makeup.
I. Identify different facial types and demonstrate procedures for basic corrective makeup.
J. Demonstrate the application and removal of artificial lashes.

VI. EXAMPLES OF ASSIGNMENTS
A. Reading Assignments
1. Read an article about infection control in a professional magazine such as American
Hairdresser, Modern Salon and Behind the Chair. Be prepared to give a three minute
presentation to the class focused on essential infection control methods.
2. Read an article from a professional magazine focused on proper (ergonomic) haircutting
techniques. Prepare a short video to demonstrate your understanding of this topic.
B. Writing Assignments
1. Write a 1-2 page summary of an interview with a sales representative from one of the
major beauty industry suppliers such as Redken, SalonCentric, CosmoProf or Sally
Beauty.
2. Write a 1-2 page essay analyzing how the economy potentially influences or impacts the
Cosmetology industry.
C. Out-of-Class Assignments
1. Interview a product representative from Redken, Dermalogica or Paul Mitchell. Be
prepared to present your findings in class.
2. Interview five salon patrons regarding what they look for in a salon, why they select
certain salons and what makes them decide to return to a salon. Summarize your findings
in a data chart or table.
