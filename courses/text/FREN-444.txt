I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
FREN 1 - First Semester French
Prerequisite(s): NONE
Recommended Prep: Reading Level III; English Level III
Transfer Status: CSU/UC
68 hours Lecture

4 Unit(s)

This course is a study of beginning French emphasizing grammar, reading, writing and speaking. It
also introduces the student to various aspects of the cultures of French-speaking regions. Students who
have completed at least one, but fewer than two years of high school French (or equivalent) with a
letter grade of “A” or “B” within one year of the present date, should register for Second Semester
French.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. recognize and begin to imitate French pronunciation
B. comprehend basic French at the introductory level
C. speak basic French at the introductory level
D. write basic French at the introductory level
E. formulate arguments supporting the importance of second language acquisition
F. appraise elements of French culture
III. COURSE CONTENT
A. Unit Titles/Suggested Time Schedule

VI. EXAMPLES OF ASSIGNMENTS
A. Reading Assignments
1. Read a diagram with statistics about recreational activities of French youth and describe
the information to a partner.
2. Read a short text about the climates of different regions of France. Afterward, create a
table with the most important information, such as high temperatures and precipitation.
B. Writing Assignments
1. Write a brief diary entry about the day's events, in order to practice the past tense.
2. Write a short message to a friend describing the classes you are taking and your favorite
subject.
C. Out-of-Class Assignments
1. Prepare outside of class an original skit based on a communicative context found in course
materials. Perform the skit in class.
2. Complete several "cloze" activities focused on accurate conjugation of verbs located in
the textbook or handout and bring to the next class.
