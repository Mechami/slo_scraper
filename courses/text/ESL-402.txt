COURSE OUTLINE
I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
ESL 234 - ESL Listening/Speaking 2
Prerequisite(s): ESL 233 or qualifying eligibility from the ESL Assessment
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
34 hours Lecture

2 Unit(s)

This course provides the non-native English speaking student the strategies and structured
opportunities to improve the oral communication necessary to succeed in academic settings. Emphasis
is on effective and appropriate communication in many practical situations, as well as pronunciation
and listening comprehension. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. respond appropriately to questions about various topics
B. discuss real-life scenarios
C. prepare and critique presentations and speeches
D. apply comments and suggestions from critiques to improve presentations
E. demonstrate correct enunciation
F. formulate effective communication in a variety of situations
