I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
COS 109 - Esthetician (Skin Care)
Prerequisite(s): High School Diploma or G.E.D.
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
110 hours Lecture
490 hours Lab

16 Unit(s)

Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be eligible to take the state board exam
and become a California state licensed Esthetician. The skin care course is an intensive course for the
student seeking an entry level position in the industry, or for a person reentering the job market with
new skills. Books and materials are to be purchased from Chico Beauty College, cost subject to
change.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Safely and accurately use all equipment and products related to the industry.
B. Demonstrate the most up-to-date methods and procedures.
C. Apply good health and safety rules.
D. Demonstrate appropriate communication skills.
E. Practice an orderly performance of all tasks and skills, establishing methodical habits without
sacrificing artistic skills.

V. METHODS OF EVALUATION
A. Weekly theory quizzes: students must maintain an average of 75% or better as recommended by
the State Board of Barbering and Cosmetology.
B. Midterm exam at 300 hours.
C. Final exam (practical and written) at 500 hours (mock state exam).
D. Projects and assignments completed.
E. Attendance: students may not miss more than thirty-two hours of class time as recommended by
the State Board of Barbering and Cosmetology.
F. Testing scores: students must maintain a 75% or average on all written and practical exams as
recommended by the State Board of Barbering and Cosmetology
