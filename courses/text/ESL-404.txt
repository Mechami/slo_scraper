I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
ESL 252 - ESL Writing Workshop
3 Unit(s)
Prerequisite(s): Qualifying eligibility for ESL Level IV based on ESL assessment or
ESL 313
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: NT
51 hours Lecture
This course provides intermediate and advanced English Language Learners with various opportunities
to develop their language skills. Reading, spelling, and vocabulary skills necessary for basic written
self-expression will be included. Pass/No Pass Only.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. Demonstrate fluency in oral and written communication.
B. Name and define basic parts of speech.
C. Describe the writing process.
D. Write simple sentences.
E. Follow directions and complete short writing assignments.
F. Recognize changes necessary to improve communication.
G. Evaluate writing samples in order to improve communication.
