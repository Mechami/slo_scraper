I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
EDUC 100 - On Course for Student Success
1 Unit(s)
Prerequisite(s): NONE
Recommended Prep: ENGL 215 and READ 223 or Reading Level II; English Level
I
Transfer Status: NT
17 hours Lecture
This first year experience student success course gives students insight into and experience with tools
and skills critical for college-level academic success.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. demonstrate the ability to independently access resources to support individual academic goals
B. identify success strategies appropriate to their learning styles
C. employ On Course concepts in their college classes
