COURSE OUTLINE
I. CATALOG DESCRIPTION
PHYS 11 - Concepts of Physics Laboratory
Prerequisite(s): PHYS 10 (or concurrent enrollment)
Recommended Prep: NONE
Transfer Status: CSU/UC
51 hours Lab

1 Unit(s)

This course is designed to accompany the lecture topics taught in Physics 10. Experiments performed
will cover the areas of motion, sound, electricity and magnetism, and light. Not recommended for
physics majors.
II. OBJECTIVES
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be able to:
A. understand the basic laws of physics through experimentation.
B. perform scientific investigation and simple report writing.
C. recognize and describe the basic laws of physics found in the physical environment and the
technological world in which they must function.
